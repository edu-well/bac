The Art of Thoughtful Discourse in the Digital Age

Understanding Pronouns and Verbs

Pronouns are words that replace nouns to avoid repetition.
In English: Pronouns include words like "he," "she," "it," "they," etc.
In Tounsi: Pronouns y3awdo issem 7atta ma n3awdouch kol marra. Mathalan: "Ali is happy. He is playing." → "Ali fer7an. Houwa yel3eb."

Verbs are action words. They tell us what the subject is doing.
In English: Verbs include "run," "eat," "write," etc.
In Tounsi: Verbs houma l'fi3el. Mathalan: "She runs every day." → "Hiya terkodh kol nhar."

Examples:

  • He eats quickly. → Houwa yakol bsur3a.
  • They are watching TV. → Houma yetferrjou fi télé.
  • It is raining. → Il meteo tzid. (Tounsi style)
  • I write stories. → Ana nektib 9issat.

Exercises:

Exercise 1: Complete with the correct pronoun:

  1. _____ is my best friend. (Ali)
  2. My sister is a doctor. _____ helps many people.
  3. This is my dog. _____ loves to play.
  4. _____ live in Tunis. (My family and I)

Answers and Explanation:

  1. He is my best friend. → Ali = He
  2. She helps many people. → Sister = She
  3. It loves to play. → Dog = It
  4. We live in Tunis. → My family and I = We

Exercise 2: Choose the correct verb form:

  1. She _____ (eat / eats) lunch at noon.
  2. They _____ (plays / play) football after school.
  3. I _____ (write / writes) a letter every week.
  4. It _____ (bark / barks) loudly.

Answers and Explanation:

  1. eats → She = singular, needs "s" with verb.
  2. play → They = plural, no "s".
  3. write → I = always use base form (no "s").
  4. barks → It = singular, needs "s".
  1. Precision over provocation: Aim for clarity rather than clickability
  2. Substance over speed: Value considered responses over immediate reactions
  3. Dialogue over diatribe: Seek understanding before victory
  4. Depth over distraction: Create content that rewards sustained attention

The future of our public discourse depends on our ability to reclaim these virtues in digital spaces. As creators, consumers, and participants in online conversations, we each have a role to play in shaping that future.
